Sustainability at Sweetgreen

We believe that healthy food should be good for you and good for the planet. Every meal we serve is part of our commitment to creating a more sustainable food system.

  • Local Sourcing

    We partner with local farms to bring you the freshest ingredients while supporting our community

  • Compostable Packaging

    All our bowls, utensils, and packaging are made from compostable materials

  • Carbon Neutral Delivery

    We offset delivery emissions and prioritize sustainable transportation options

  • Regenerative Agriculture

    Supporting farming practices that restore soil health and biodiversity
